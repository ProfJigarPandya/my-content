Primitives meaning which are used during early stage of data mining. 
These include
- Manage to have task relevant data
- Be known to the matter what are you looking for. Sometimes, this can be altogether unknown.
- Any relevant/domain specific information/knowledge about the task which can help during initial and matured discovery process
- Have rough idea about thresholds when required to be discarded or to keep certain outcome
- Look for means to represent findings in various visual and effective ways.

Take for example, mining for user better experience/less complaints, data analysts are required to perform above steps to start with.
