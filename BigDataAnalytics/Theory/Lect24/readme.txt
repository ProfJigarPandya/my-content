There are design principles involved in creating visualizations in data analytics presentation phase. One has to avoid misleading or deceptive charts because it will definitely adversely impact on decisions made and the final outcome will harm one or other.


Do NOT cut off y-axis.

Do NOT use a dual-y axis.

Do NOT artificially limit the scope of the data.

Do NOT disguise important trends and insights by problematic choices in how data is binned or grouped.

Do NOT use pie-chart to showcase part-to-whole where totals do not sum up to 100% appropriate.

Do NOT hide trends via showcasing cumulative charts.

Do NOT artificially smooth data and hence trends.
